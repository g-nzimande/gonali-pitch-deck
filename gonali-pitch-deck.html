✅
Quality Sourcing Only

No grey imports, no counterfeit parts. Gonali stocks OEM-equivalent and certified aftermarket brands only — Mann, Bosch, Brembo, NGK, Castrol, Willard. Every SKU is traceable to a verified SA distributor.

Risk & Mitigation
We've thought hard about
what could go wrong.
RISK 01 · HIGH
Parts Theft in Transit
📦

Drivers could steal stock or be robbed en route, particularly for high-value parts like batteries and brake sets.

MITIGATION
→GPS live tracking every 30s. Deviation from route triggers an alert
→Tamper-evident sealed packaging — open parcel = refused delivery
→Orders over R800 require OTP or photo signature at door
→Goods-in-transit insurance on all deliveries
→Drivers carry no cash — 100% digital removes robbery incentive
RISK 02 · HIGH
Driver Trustworthiness
🪪

Unvetted drivers create theft, safety, and reputational risk. SA's gig economy has had publicised driver misconduct incidents.

MITIGATION
→SAPS criminal background clearance — mandatory before first shift
→Valid SA driver's licence + ID document verification
→2 contactable personal references
→30-day probationary period with a senior driver mentor
→Zero-tolerance policy — theft = immediate termination + criminal charges
RISK 03 · MEDIUM
Wrong Part Delivered
⚠️

Incorrect parts could be fitted, cause vehicle damage, or in safety-critical cases, contribute to an accident.

MITIGATION
→Fitment database blocks checkout if part is incompatible
→Safety-critical parts display professional fitment advisory
→48-hour return policy on any unopened, incompatible part
→Product liability insurance in place from day one
RISK 04 · MEDIUM
Incumbent Response
🏢

AutoZone or Midas could attempt to launch a competing same-day service after seeing early Gonali traction.

MITIGATION
→Incumbents are retail-first — logistics pivot requires full restructuring
→Sixty60 had 4+ years before credible competitors emerged
→We race to depot density and trade account lock-in before they react
→Brand loyalty and NPS-driven retention creates switching cost
RISK 05 · MANAGED
Inventory & Cash Flow
📊

Holding inventory ties up capital. Overstocking slow-moving parts creates dead stock. Understocking causes SLA failures.

MITIGATION
→Start with a curated 80-SKU catalogue of highest-frequency parts
→Reorder alerts built into warehouse system at configurable thresholds
→Negotiate consignment terms with 1–2 key distributors at launch
→Order data from week 1 informs replenishment — we learn fast
RISK 06 · LOW
Regulatory & Compliance
⚖️

Operating as a courier and parts retailer brings regulatory obligations around labour, tax, and road transport.

MITIGATION
→Drivers employed (not gig) — UIF, PAYE compliant from launch
→Registered as a Pty Ltd with VAT registration at R1M threshold
→Retail operating licence per depot — standard retail requirement
→Legal counsel retained for B-BBEE compliance and employment contracts

Social Impact
Jobs for South African
youth. By design.
The Context

South Africa's youth unemployment rate sits at ~45%. The SA delivery industry is dominated by foreign nationals — often because formal employment pathways for young South Africans in logistics are limited. Gonali changes this by design.

The Gonali Driver Academy
🎓
Structured Onboarding Programme

Every new driver completes a formal programme covering: road safety, customer service excellence, route optimisation, basic automotive product knowledge, and financial literacy (managing income, tax, savings).

📜
SETA-Aligned Learnership

In partnership with the Transport SETA, graduates receive a recognised learnership certificate — transferable to any logistics employer in SA. Gonali contributes to SETA levy obligations while creating real skills.

📈
Clear Progression Pathway

Driver → Senior Driver (6 months) → Depot Coordinator (18 months) → Warehouse Supervisor. Gonali is not a dead-end gig — it's a career entry point in the automotive supply chain.

How We Attract SA Youth
🤝
Community Partnership Recruitment

We recruit directly through FET/TVET colleges, community organisations, and church networks in our depot suburbs — Soweto (JHB South), Mitchells Plain (CPT), KwaMashu (DBN). Local knowledge = better delivery performance.

💊
Proper Employment, Not Gig

Gonali drivers are employed — not independent contractors. UIF, PAYE, and basic conditions of employment apply from day one. This is a deliberate differentiator from Uber Eats and Mr D's model, and qualifies Gonali for B-BBEE employment equity points.

🏆
Performance Culture

Monthly performance bonuses tied to on-time delivery rate and customer rating. Top performers are publicly recognised as "Gonali Champions" — creating pride and retention. Drivers who know their neighbourhoods earn more by serving them better.
